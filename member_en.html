Principal Investigator

Songlin Li

Associate Professor, Doctoral supervisor, Thousand Youth Talents Plan of 2016. He received his bachelor's degree and master's degree from Chongqing University in 2003 and 2006, and his doctor's degree from Institute of Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2009. From 2009 to 2016, he worked in National Institute of Materials Science in Japan and University of Strasbourg in France, respectively. Presiding over / participating in General Program of National Natural Science Foundation of China and National Key R&D Program of China, etc., and publishing over 80 papers in high-level journals, e.g., Nat. Commun., Nano Lett., ACS Nano, Adv. Mater., Chem. Soc. Rev., etc., with more than 4000 SCI citations and an H-INDEX of 30.
