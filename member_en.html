Principal Investigator

Songlin Li

1999-2006 Department of Physics, Chongqing University, CN Bachelor, Master
2006-2009 Institute of Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, CN Ph.D.
2009-2011 National Institute for Materials Science, JP Postdoc
2011-2014 National Institute for Materials Science, JP ICYS Fellow (Tenure Track)
2014-2016 University of Strabourg, FR Postdoc
2016-present Nanjing University, CN Associate Professor

In recent years, the research interests are focused on the field of two-dimensional materials for electronic and information devices. Realization of graphene voltage inverter with matched input and output voltages for the first time, and it was highly appraised in the development roadmap of the European "Graphene Flagship"; Development of universal physical scattering model for two-dimensional atomic crystal based transistors, to clarify the thickness dependence of the channel mobility. Presiding over General Program of National Natural Science Foundation of China, etc., and publishing over 80 papers in high-level journals, e.g., Chem. Soc. Rev., Nano Lett., Adv. Mater., ACS Nano, etc., with more than 4000 SCI citations and an H-INDEX of 30.
