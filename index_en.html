Welcome to LiLab

  Welcome to Songlin Li's Group at School of Electronic Science and Engineering, Nanjing University (China). The current research interests of our research group focus on the field of two-dimensional materials, including basic research on electrical transport of two-dimensional materials, device and process optimization, short channel and ballistic transport, research and development of new processes and structures, etc.
