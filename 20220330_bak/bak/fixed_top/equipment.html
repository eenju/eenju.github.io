Group Introduction

Welcome to Prof. Songlin Li's group at Nanjing University. Our current research program focuses on the synthesis, properties and device applications of low-dimensional materials. We are interested in a wide range of atomic and molecular crystals as well as their hybrids, such as graphene, carbon nanotube and MoS2.
